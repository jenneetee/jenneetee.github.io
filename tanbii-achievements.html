TANBII INTERNSHIP PROJECTS

Internship Summary

Programs Used: Figma, Google Slides, Google Doc

As a Game UI/UX Design intern at Tanbii, I collaborated with a team to research and propose enhancements to various sections of the Tanbii app. My contributions included developing ideas to refine the Achievements menu, improving the Carbon Tracking menu, and conceptualizing a "Refer a Player" feature.

Our workflow began with a thorough analysis of the existing UI, followed by research into similar apps to gather inspiration and best practices. We then created wireframes using Figma to visualize our concepts. After refining these designs, we developed final prototypes that were presented to our supervisor and stakeholders.
